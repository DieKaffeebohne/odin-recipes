Sous Vide Garlic Chicken Thighs

Description

The following recipe is meant as a starter meal for first time users of the Sous Vide.

Ingredients
  • Raw,bone-in,skinless chicken Thighs
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
Steps
  1. Rinse chicken and season with salt and pepper
  2. Vaccuum seal chicken in bag with fresh garlic cloves
  3. Cook in Sous Vide at 250 degrees for 10 hours
  4. Remove chicken from bag and quickly sear both sides on cast iron skillet
  5. Enjoy!
